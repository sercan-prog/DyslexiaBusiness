Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 02, 2026

Welcome to Dyslexia Business Pro. Your privacy is critically important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when using our mobile application.

1. Overview

Dyslexia Business Pro is designed as a Privacy-First assistant. Our primary goal is to process data locally on your device whenever possible to ensure maximum security for business professionals.

2. Data Collection & Permissions

  • Camera: Used strictly for scanning printed documents (OCR). Images are processed on-device and are not stored on our servers.
  • Microphone: Used for real-time speech-to-text recording during meetings. Audio files are processed locally and not uploaded externally unless explicitly requested for AI analysis.
  • Text Analysis: Any text processed for dyslexia corrections or professional tone polishing is handled securely. We use local NLP engines for basic corrections and industry-standard AI APIs for advanced polishing.

3. Local Storage

Your personalized word history (Adaptive Learning Profile) is stored locally on your device using secure storage. This data never leaves your device and is used only to improve your personal correction experience.

4. Third-Party Services

We use a limited number of trusted third-party services to provide core app functionality:

  • RevenueCat: Used for managing your Pro subscription and verifying purchase status.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play: Handles all payment transactions securely.
  • AI Providers: Advanced tone polishing features use industry-standard AI models. Data sent to these services is used only for real-time analysis and is not stored permanently.

5. Your Rights

Since most of your data is stored locally, you have full control over it. You can clear your word history or reset your settings at any time within the app's settings menu.
